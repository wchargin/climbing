It helps to take a look at this route ahead of time: (a) the two black holds on the right side of the higher gray volume are key, but you can't see them on the wall, and (b) it's useful to know that it traverses so far to the right. When I cleared this, I didn't end up using the farthest-right hold; I didn't trust my lean and opted to just bump to the finish.
