The bottom-right polytopes are purple, not volumes. Once established on the left "forward slash" volume, lay back on it with both hands near the top so that you can stand with right foot on the black "backslash" hold. The top-left hold just above the crease is black; don't miss it! The top-right hold on the top face is not great to grab to.
