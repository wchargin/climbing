Route #98

2023-05-30 · SBP Poplar

Started 2023-05-29 and got real close (piecewise cleared) but exhausted; finished 2023-05-30 first try. Beta from a friendly fellow climber: the kneebar on the big underhold is excellent.

This is a fun route! After starting facing out from the two lowest volumes, you have to dynamically barndoor to the right wall. I then heel-hook up onto the middle-right volume so that I can grab to the pinches without flailing too wildly. Once you get to the kneebar, it's no surprises, just exerting.
