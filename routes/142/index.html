Black #106

Route #142

2023-10-06 · SBP Poplar

Fun black in the southeast corner of the northern room. Feels good on the body to climb. I always like this kind of volume, really accommodating to grip along its length and also at its top. You don't have to, but you can press your right knee and thigh into its right face when grabbing up to the first large hold. The gap between the two mid-high holds right next to each other comfortably accommodates hand or foot, and the last two holds are kind, too.
