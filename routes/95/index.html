Blue #10

Route #95

2023-05-29 · SBP Poplar

Hard start, jumping to that pointy volume! You can either do both hands on the vertex or left hand on the vertex and right hand on the smaller volume atop it. Helps to lean right as you pull up so that you can better slam your foot into the right side of that volume. Once you're up on top of it, it's a precarious and surprisingly taxing balancing game to get to the finish. Difficult but fun climb.
