2024-01-26 · SBP Poplar

I had tried this maybe a week ago and gotten really stuck on the start. The polyhedral volume-looking things to the right of the start hold are, according to the consensus of folks with standard color vision, decidedly purple and not volumes, but they look close enough to volumes to me that I was brain-stuck on trying to go up in that general direction. But then I saw someone doing it by using the fun hexagonal volumes to the left, and a few days later I tried that approach and was able to clear it on the first such try. It's not easy, and the grips are not accommodating (very slopey), but I enjoyed it!
