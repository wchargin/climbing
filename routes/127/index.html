Poked at the start: the angle on the big volume directly beneath the start hold is very shallow, and it's easy to slide off; I didn't attempt to start the route. Then I flashed it when I saw someone trying it out by pointing the toe of their left foot right into the volume, along the top edge maybe 60% of the way to the right. The end hold is very good in the way that it needs to be; you can grab behind it and hold on easily.
