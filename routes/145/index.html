Route #145

2023-10-11 · SBP Poplar

Start with what's mostly a left-foot pistol squat, with some assistance from right foot as it moves up the wall. Then, proceed with either a reverse inside flag or a reverse outside flag. You can use a reverse outdoor flag grabbing to the side-facing hold, which requires some care about barn-dooring but is quite solid once established. Or, you can use a reverse inside flag, which makes the next move harder! (I felt like I had to kind of dyno to the finish.) The tiny platform on the on the bottom-right volume is quite useful.

Also, you can bump your head on the green pocket if you're not careful.
