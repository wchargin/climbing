Route #170

2023-12-07 · SBP Poplar

Cool start on this one: the top start hold has barely any lip, so I do a kind of crossed-hand start facing right with right hand on the upper hold and left hand pressing into the lower hold. Establishing onto the four holds in the lower-right is doable enough; I think the crux is reaching to the high hold directly above them, whether dynamically or statically. I've done it statically, and tried it dynamically once but didn't stick it. It feels scary for some reason!

Once you get to that high hold, it's not too hard to step onto the left volume and traverse over to the end. The finish hold is good enough.
