Vital Red #8

Route #32

2022-06-08 · Vital Brooklyn

Black holds. Downstairs, wall facing the gym. The hardest part is establishing (though the very end is somewhat tricky). Approach: left foot on foothold, right foot just toe on wall, both hands grabbing left end of start hold and kind of pushing up and into it while getting mass as close to wall as possible; stabilize briefly and then grab with left hand, then switch feet left. Want to work on getting to the final hold; there's not a great place to push down into. Definitely used the bolt hole to good effect on my first clear.
